The Problem

Airlines Are Leaving Money on the Table

Fuel accounts for 25-35% of operating costs. Yet most airlines lack the tools to optimize it route-by-route.

💸

$200K+ Legacy Software

SITA OptiFlight, Honeywell GoDirect cost $200K+/year. Small and mid-size airlines can't afford them.

📊

No Route-Level Visibility

Airlines know total fuel burn but can't see which specific routes, altitudes, or speed choices waste fuel.

🌍

CORSIA 2027 Deadline

All international flights must report and offset emissions. Most operators aren't ready for MRV compliance.

Our Solution

4-Axis Fuel & Emission Optimization

Physics-based analysis using ICAO ISA atmosphere model, drag polars, and BFFM2 emission methodology. No black boxes. Full transparency.

Cruise Altitude Optimization

Find the optimal FL for every weight and wind condition. Includes buffet margin (FAR 25.251) and RVSM compliance.

Step Climb Planning

As fuel burns, optimal altitude rises. Our model plans 2-4 step climbs with net fuel saving calculations.

Cost Index / Speed Optimization

Route-specific CI tables balance fuel cost vs time cost. One CI doesn't fit all routes.

Fuel Tankering Analysis

When origin fuel is cheaper, carry extra. We calculate the exact profitable amount including carriage penalty.
